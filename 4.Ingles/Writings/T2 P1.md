When it comes to taking part into an activity, many people argue about whether they should do it alone or with other people involved. When talking to parents, most of them will tell you that, for their children, having activities with other children of it's age is important for their development in life, so they could learn how to communicate with other people and work together at solving difficult problems that, solving them on their own, would take much more time. 

Not only this skill is important when working in the school but also at sport like activities. Here is where the sportsmanship bright the most, when different people work together in order to achieve the same goal, generally winning a competition.
